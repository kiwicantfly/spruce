How to play ?

You must keep all the letters from the previous word, then rearrange them and add a new letter to form the next word.

Your spruce will grow until you give up or the computer fails at finding a new word.

Example :

a

an

man

name

named

You can use plurals and conjugated verbs.

Your score will get better as you find longer words, change the order of the letters and use infrequent letters.

Need help ? Click on the word above the keyboard to shuffle the letters.
